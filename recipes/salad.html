Ceasar salad

Description

This Caesar salad recipe is guaranteed to impress dinner party guests! It's topped with homemade croutons, crisp veggies, and a rich, tangy dressing.

Ingredients

  • Lettuce
  • Cheese
  • Homemade croutons
  • Ceasar dressing

Steps

  1. Chop your lettuce into a bowl.
  2. Scatter the cheese into the bowl
  3. Add in the bread croutons.
  4. Add a little bit of the dressing.
  5. Mix everything and vuola.
