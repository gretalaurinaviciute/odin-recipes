Avocado toast

Description

Creamy avocado spread onto well-toasted bread is one of my favorite quick meals.

Ingredients

  • An avocado
  • Whole grain bread
  • Pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Mash your avocado.
  2. Mix in your salt.
  3. Toast the bread.
  4. Spread the mashed avocado on the bread.
  5. Enjoy.
