Pancakes

Description

This simple recipe will become your go-to for making fluffy and delicious pancakes from scratch.

You will only need a few ingredients that you probably already have in your house and it will take you approximately 30 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup of flour
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 a teaspoon of sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat your pan.
  2. Peel your banana and put it in a blender.
  3. Crack the egg and pour in the milk.
  4. Mix all the wet ingredients.
  5. Pour in the flour little by little.
  6. When the batter is ready and the pan is hot enough, you are ready to cook the pancakes.
